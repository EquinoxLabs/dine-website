‘Safe Place to Dine’ makes it easy to survey your restaurant’s safety and hygiene standards and get recognized for your safe dining culture. It is a definitive recognition to identify and acknowledge the efforts of the restaurants in keeping their customers safe. ‘Safe Place to Dine’ leverages the best auditing parameters curated by the industry experts to quantify the current safety and hygiene standards of your restaurants. The certification will prove instrumental in cultivating credibility among customers as well as other stakeholders.

Restaurants invest a lot of their time, money, and efforts to ensure that they offer the best safety and hygiene standards to their customers and these endeavors deserve recognition. Thus, this assessment process reveals key factors that help in defining and evaluating whether the safety and hygiene standards are conformed to the specified guidelines. Restaurants and even their food delivery services are audited based on their level of compliance and accordingly awarded with a ‘Safe Place to Dine’ certificate, which is recognized nationwide.
